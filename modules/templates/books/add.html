You can add/register books either by importing a file (must be an excel or csv file) or by filling in the book inventory form. You can download a sample of the import file.
The excel or csv file should have these column titles at the top row: Classification No., Title, Author, Category, Publication, Quantity Added, Catalogue No., and Comments.
Note: Catalogue No. and Comments are not required.
